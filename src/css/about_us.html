About QMTIS

QMTIS is the most prestigious student-managed Finance & Investment Club at Queen Mary, University of London. Our mission is to allow students to develop a certain interest and a broader and more in-depth knowledge of everything there is around the world of finance and investing. We pursue our mission by connecting our members with companies and professional through events, activities, and workshops.
